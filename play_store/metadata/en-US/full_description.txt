Member Directory is a simple, private directory designed for community groups, senior centers and associations.

Manage member details directly on your device:

• search by name, phone number or membership card;
• call a member with one tap;
• save up to two phone numbers;
• record membership number, expiry date, birthday and notes;
• import Excel (.xlsx) and CSV lists;
• export and share the entire directory between your devices;
• customize the name of your center or association.

The app works completely offline, with no account and no server. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to export and share a backup.

Member Directory is free, ad-free and open source.
